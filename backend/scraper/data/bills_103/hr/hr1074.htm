                                 A BILL


 
 To suspend until January 1, 1997, the duty on mounted closed circuit 
                           television lenses.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. MOUNTED CLOSED CIRCUIT TELEVISION LENSES.

    Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of 
the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the
