                                 A BILL


 
To provide for the separate administration of the Border Patrol and the 
                Immigration and Naturalization Service.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ADMINISTRATION OF THE BORDER PATROL.

    Effective October 1, 1993, the border patrol of the Immigration and 
Naturalization Service shall be administered through the Department of 
Justice under the Attorney General and shall not be part of, or 
administered through, the Immigration and Naturalization Service.
