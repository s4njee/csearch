                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 10, United States Code, to allow members of the Selected 
      Reserve to use educational assistance for graduate programs.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE FOR GRADUATE PROGRAMS FOR MEMBERS OF 
              THE SELECTED RESERVE.

    Section 2131(c)(1) of title 10, United States Code, is amended by 
striking out ``other than'' and all that follows through ``level.'' and 
inserting in lieu thereof a period.
