                                 A BILL


 
                 For the relief of Peter J. Montagnoli.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That the Secretary of 
the Treasury is authorized and directed to pay, out of any money in the 
Treasury not otherwise appropriated, to Peter J. Montagnoli of Las 
Vegas, Nevada, the sum of $8,000, in full satisfaction of all his 
claims against the United States for reimbursement for the expenses he 
incurred from July 1970 through May 7, 1973, in securing the 
reimbursement of his disability insurance benefits under the Social 
Security Act which were terminated in September 1970.
