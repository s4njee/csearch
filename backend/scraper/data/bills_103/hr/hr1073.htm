                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 to exempt from 
 coverage under that Act individuals who are members of the Old Order 
                                 Amish.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXEMPTION.

    Section 3(6) of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (29 
U.S.C. 652(6) is amended by inserting before the period the following: 
``but does not include an individual who is a member of the Old Order 
Amish''.
