                                 A BILL


 
  To improve the environmental protection of Barnegat Bay, New Jersey.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Barnegat Bay Marine Environmental 
Improvement Act''.

SEC. 2. AMENDMENT.

    Section 320(a)(2)(B) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (33 
U.S.C. 1330(a)(2)(B)) is amended by striking ``and Peconic Bay, New 
York'' and inserting instead ``Peconic Bay, New York; and Barnegat Bay, 
New Jersey''.
