                                 A BILL


 
 To provide that any foreign nation that conducts a test of a nuclear 
  weapon in the United States shall pay the costs resulting from the 
                                 test.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PAYMENT OF COSTS RESULTING FROM CERTAIN NUCLEAR WEAPONS 
              TESTING.

    Any foreign nation that conducts a test of a nuclear weapon in the 
United States shall pay the costs associated with the test, including 
the environmental restoration costs as determined by the Administrator 
of the Environmental Protection Agency.
