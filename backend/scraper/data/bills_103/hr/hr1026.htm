                                 A BILL


 
  To repeal the first section of Public Law 93-462 to limit departing 
  Members' purchases of office equipment and office furnishings from 
                        their district offices.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That the first section 
of the Act entitled ``An Act to authorize the disposition of certain 
office equipment and furnishings, and for other purposes'' (Public Law 
93-462; 88 Stat. 1388; 2 U.S.C. 59a) is repealed.
