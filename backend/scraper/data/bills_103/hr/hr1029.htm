                                 A BILL


 
 To provide for an increase in personnel of the Border Patrol, and for 
                            other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. INCREASE IN PERSONNEL OF THE BORDER PATROL.

    There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be 
necessary to provide for an authorized personnel level of 6,600 for the 
border patrol of the United States.
