                                 A BILL


 
    To reduce until January 1, 1995, the duty on succinic anhydride.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SUCCINIC ANHYDRIDE.

    Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of 
the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the 
following new subheading:

      

       ``  9902.31.12  Succinic anhydride, not derived in whole or in part from                    No change        No change        On or before 12/

                        2917.19.27).............................................  4%                                                                  . 

SEC. 2. APPLICABILITY.

    In General.--The amendment made by section 1 applies with respect 
to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or 
after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
