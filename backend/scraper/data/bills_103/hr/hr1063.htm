                                 A BILL


 
 To authorize appropriations for the National Historical Publications 
 and Records Commission for fiscal year 1994 through fiscal year 1999.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION OF APPROPRIATIONS.

    Paragraph (1) of subsection (f) of section 2504 of title 44, United 
States Code, is amended--
            (1) in subparagraph (B), by striking out ``and'' after the 
        semicolon;
            (2) in subparagraph (C), by striking out the period and 
        inserting in lieu thereof a semicolon; and
            (3) by adding at the end the following new subparagraphs:
                    ``(D) $12,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1994 
                and 1995;
                    ``(E) $15,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1996 
                and 1997; and
                    ``(F) $18,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1998 
                and 1999.''.

SEC. 2. SEPARATE APPROPRIATION REQUIREMENT.

    Subsection (f) of section 2504 of title 44, United States Code, is 
amended by adding at the end the following new paragraph:
    ``(3) Amounts authorized to be appropriated under this subsection 
shall be appropriated separately from amounts appropriated for the 
National Archives and Records Administration.''.
