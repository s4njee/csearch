                                 A BILL


 
   To extend the existing suspension of duty on tetraamino biphenyl.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF EXISTING SUSPENSION OF DUTY.

    Heading 9902.29.27 (relating to tetraamino biphenyl) of the 
Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended by striking 
out ``12/31/92'' and inserting ``12/31/95''.

SEC. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.

    The amendment made by section 1 applies to goods entered, or 
withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, after December 31, 1992.
