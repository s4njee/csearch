                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit government-to-government and commercial arms sales to any 
  country that is participating in or cooperating with the boycott of 
                       Israel by Arab countries.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Arab Boycott Arms Sales Prohibition 
Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION ON ARMS SALES TO COUNTRIES PARTICIPATING IN OR 
              COOPERATING WITH THE ARAB BOYCOTT OF ISRAEL.

    (a) Government-To-Government Sales.--Sales may not be made under 
the Arms Export Control Act to any country described in subsection (c).
    (b) Commercial Export Sales.--Licenses may not be issued under 
section 38 of the Arms Export Control Act with respect to a sale to any 
country described in subsection (c).
    (c) Countries Subject to Prohibitions.--This section applies with 
respect to any country that is listed pursuant to section 999(a)(3) of 
the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 as a country which requires or may 
require participation in or cooperation with the boycott of Israel by 
Arab countries.
