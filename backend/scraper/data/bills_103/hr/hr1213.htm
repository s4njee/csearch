                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to include veterans 
participating in Operation Desert Storm and other veterans as eligible 
                 for veterans' mortgage bond financing.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. OPERATION DESERT STORM VETERANS ETC., ELIGIBLE FOR VETERANS' 
              MORTGAGE BOND FINANCING.

    (a) In General.--Subparagraph (A) of section 143(l)(4) of the 
Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (defining qualified veteran) is amended 
by striking ``before January 1, 1977'' and inserting ``on or before the 
date designated by the President for purposes of section 112 as the 
date of the termination of combatant activities in the Persian Gulf 
region.''
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) shall 
apply to financing provided after the date of the enactment of this 
Act.
