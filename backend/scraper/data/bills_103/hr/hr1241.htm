                                 A BILL


 
 To amend title 18, United States Code, to provide for increase reward 
                  amounts in domestic terrorist cases.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Domestic Anti-Terrorist Reward Act 
of 1993''.

SEC. 2. TITLE 18 AMENDMENT.

    Chapter 204 of title 18, United States Code, is amended--
            (1) in section 3072, by striking ``$500,000'' and inserting 
        ``$2,000,000''; and
            (2) in section 3075, by striking ``$5,000,000'' and 
        inserting ``$10,000,000''.
