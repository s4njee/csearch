                                 A BILL


 
  To authorize appropriations for the Federal Election Commission for 
                           fiscal year 1994.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That the second 
sentence of section 314 of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971 (2 
U.S.C. 439c) is amended by striking out ``and'' after ``1978,'' and by 
inserting after ``1981'' the following: ``, and $23,654,000 for the 
fiscal year ending September 30, 1994''.
