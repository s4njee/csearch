                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 with respect to the 
      eligibility of veterans for mortgage revenue bond financing.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That (a) paragraph (4) 
of section 143(l) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (defining 
qualified veteran) is amended to read as follows:
            ``(4) Qualified veteran.--For purposes of this subsection, 
        the term `qualified veteran' means any veteran who meets such 
        requirements as may be imposed by the State law pursuant to 
        which qualified veterans' mortgage bonds are issued.''
    (b) The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to obligations 
issued after the date of the enactment of this Act.
