                                 A BILL


 
To amend the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993 to restore the leave 
                      rights of certain employees.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. RESTORATION OF LEAVE RIGHTS.

    (a) In General.--Section 104 of the Family and Medical Leave Act of 
1993 (29 U.S.C. 2614) is amended by striking out subsection (b) and 
redesignating subsection (c) as subsection (b).
    (b) Conforming Amendment.--Section 502 of the Family and Medical 
Leave Act of 1993 (2 U.S.C. 60n) is amended by striking out ``(other 
than section 104(b))''.
