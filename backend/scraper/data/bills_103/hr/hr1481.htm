                                 A BILL


 
    To deauthorize the Kissimmee River restoration project, Florida.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled, That the project for 
the ecosystem restoration of the Kissimmee River, Florida, authorized 
by section 101(8) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1992 (106 
Stat. 4802), is not authorized after the date of the enactment of this 
Act.
