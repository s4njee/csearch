                                 A BILL


 
              To repeal the Service Contract Act of 1965.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF SERVICE CONTRACT ACT OF 1965.

    (a) Repeal.--The Service Contract Act of 1965 (41 U.S.C. 351 et 
seq.) is repealed.
    (b) Savings.--Notwithstanding subsection (a), the Service Contract 
Act of 1965 (41 U.S.C. 351 et seq.), as in effect before the date of 
the enactment of this Act, shall apply to a contract entered into 
before the date of the enactment of this Act as if this section were 
not enacted.
