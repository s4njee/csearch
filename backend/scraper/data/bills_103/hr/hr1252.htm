                                 A BILL


 
 To repeal title X of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control 
                              Act of 1974.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEALER.

    (a) Repealer.--Title X of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment 
Control Act of 1974 is repealed.
    (b) Conforming Amendments.--(1) Section 1(a) of the Congressional 
Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974 relating to title X is 
amended by striking ``and title X may be cited as the `Impoundment 
Control Act of 1974'''.
    (2) That portion of the table of contents set forth in section 1(b) 
of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974 
relating to title X is repealed.
    (3) Paragraph (3) of section 2 of the Congressional Budget and 
Impoundment Control Act of 1974 is repealed and paragraphs (4) and (5) 
of that section are redesignated as paragraphs (3) and (4), 
respectively.
