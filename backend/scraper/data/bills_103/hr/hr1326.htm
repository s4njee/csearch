                                 A BILL


 
      To suspend temporarily the duty on rifabutin (dosage form).

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TEMPORARY SUSPENSION OF DUTY.

    Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of 
the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the 
following new heading:

      

       ``  9902.31.14  Rifabutin (dosage form) (CAS No. 72559-06-9) (provided                      No change        No change        On or before 12/   
                        for in subheading 3004.20.00)...........................  Free                                                31/94           ''

SEC. 4. EFFECTIVE DATE.

    The amendment made by section 1 applies with respect to articles 
entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 
15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
