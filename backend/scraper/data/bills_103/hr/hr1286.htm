                                 A BILL


 
  To reduce the amount authorized for the Official Mail Allowance for 
         Members of the House of Representatives by 20 percent.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REDUCTION.

    Section 311(e)(2)(B)(i) of the Legislative Branch Appropriations 
Act, 1991 (2 U.S.C. 59e(e)(2)(B)(i)) is amended by striking ``3'' and 
inserting ``2.4''.

SEC. 2. APPLICABILITY.

    The amendment made by this Act shall apply with respect to sessions 
of Congress beginning with the first regular session of Congress 
beginning after the date of the enactment of this Act.
